Welcome to Hosanna Art



The mission of Hosanna Art is to share Gospel paired with a related work of art and

a short reflection. Our goal is to help people grow closer to God through the

magnificent pairing of art and the Christian faith.

Gospel-related events through the ages are portrayed in artwork

and photos that can be used as teaching tools.

This vast visual collection offers a wide range of scriptural themes,

doctrines, and stories that can be taught in lessons.
